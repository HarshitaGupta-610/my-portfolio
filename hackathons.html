🚀 Hackathons

My Hackathon Journey

Some challenges, some wins — but always learning something new.

IEEE Hackathon

Our idea, SheLearns, aimed to bridge the digital gap for rural women by providing offline-friendly tech education. While it was one of our first hackathons and we didn’t go too far, it helped us gain valuable exposure and confidence.

🔗 View Project on Devfolio
LeanIn Hacks

Our idea FeelWell aims to support people facing anxiety or mood swings through mood-based music, AI chats, and stress-relief features. Though we didn’t reach the final round, we gained hands-on experience in Figma and UX thinking.

🔗 View Project on Devfolio
HackJMI

We proposed an app to simplify Delhi Metro travel — showing live crowd data and enabling seat booking in advance. It was a great learning experience in real-world problem-solving and teamwork.

Google Girl Hackathon

As part of the Google Girl Hackathon, I learnt how to build impactful resumes and participated in virtual cohorts. It was also my first experience giving an online coding test on a global platform — a great confidence booster!

India's First Virtual 2D Environment Hackathon

Reached the Finalist stage and built CellMate, an AI-powered biology tutor designed to make complex concepts easier through interactive support.

Ideat-A-Thon - Chandigarh University

Proposed FitEase, a smart wellness app focused on healthy habits through gentle reminders, mood tracking, and food-label scanning. Selected for Round 2, it was a valuable experience in solving real-world healthcare problems with an empathetic, user-centric approach.

MHTechin Innovation Challenge 2025

Proposed the idea of HealSync, a smart healthcare platform that streamlines appointment booking with real-time updates, QR-based check-ins, and AI-driven emergency slot allocation. Reached the Semi-Finals at the MHTechin Innovation Challenge 2025, gaining hands-on experience in solving real-world healthcare accessibility challenges.

NationBuilding Case Study Competition 2026

Proposed the idea of WealthyHealth, a preventive healthcare platform that promotes healthy habits through micro-actions, risk tracking, and personalized nudges to improve long-term well-being. Qualified for the Presentation Round in the NationBuilding Case Study Competition 2026 after clearing the initial quiz round.
